## Specifications

- **Size:** 100x100px to 200x200px
- **Format:** PNG or SVG preferred
- **Background:** Transparent when possible
- **Naming:** Use lowercase (e.g., openai.png, microsoft.png)

## Tips

- Most companies provide official logos in their brand kits
- Vector formats (SVG) are preferred for scalability
- Ensure logos are recognizable at small sizes
- Use official brand colors/versions
